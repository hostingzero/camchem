Top 10 Chemical Companies in the World

Tina Wu - May 11, 2023

We discuss the top ten chemical companies that are providing chemical services to the world. They make a wide presence and generate revenues in billions through their chemical sales. Here is the list of the 10 best chemical companies with higher revenue in the world.

According to the latest report from ICIS, the top 10 chemical companies in the world by revenue are:

Top 10 Chemical Companies

  1. BASF SE
  2. Dow Chemical Company
  3. Sinopec Group
  4. Saudi Basic Industries Corporation
  5. EXxonMobil Corporation
  6. China National Chemical Corporation (ChemChina)
  7. LyondellBasell Industries N.V.
  8. DuPont de Nemours, Inc.
  9. Mitsubishi Chemical Holdings Corporation
  10. Sumitomo Chemical Co., Ltd.

Below we provide more detail on each of the companies.

1. BASF SE

BASF SE is a European multinational company and also the largest chemical producer in the world. BASF SE headquarters are located in Ludwigshafen, Germany. In 1912 they started manufacturing chemicals and have their customers in more than 190 countries. It has not a good environmental record due to this, it also gets some criticism.

BASF SE expanded its ventures in 80 countries and now has a name as the biggest chemical producer in the world. They are dealing with many products such as chemicals, plastics, oil & gas, performance chemicals, catalysts, coatings, crop technology, crude oil, and natural gas exploration and production worldwide. They get the largest revenue of 79.7$ billion in 2022 by dealing with their products which is why BASF SE is on the top of the list.

Check the BASF SE website for more: https://www.basf.com/ 

2. Dow Chemical Company

The Dow Chemical Company is an American multinational corporation, and its headquarter is in Midland, Michigan, United States. This company comes in the three largest chemical producers in the world. They have a presence in more than 160 countries. Dow is also a member of the American Chemistry Council.

Dow was founded in 1897 and since then they have been dealing with chemical products and have sales worldwide. Dow is a large producer of plastics, chemicals, performance chemicals, catalysts, coatings, and Hydrocarbon exploration products in many countries. Dow is the second top chemical company as they have the revenue of their sales of $45.9 billion in 2022.

Check Dow Chemical Company website: https://www.dow.com/

3. Sinopec Group

Sinopec Group also has the name China Petrochemical Corporation, it is considered the world's largest oil refining, gas, and petrochemical conglomerate. Their headquarters are at Chaoyangmenwai in Beijing. Sinopec was founded in 1998, and for 25 years they provided their chemical manufacturing and dealing in several countries. They have the name because of their diversity in product manufacturing.

Sinopec Ltd. is involved in the production of many products, for example, petroleum products, including gasoline, diesel, jet fuel, kerosene, ethylene, synthetic fibers, synthetic rubber, synthetic resins, and chemical fertilizers, also the exploration of crude oil and natural gas within China. They generated $42.4 billion in revenue through their products in 2022.

Check the Sinopec Group website for more: http://www.sinopecgroup.com/group/en/

4. Saudi Basic Industries Corporation (SABIC)

Saudi Basic Industries Corporation is also a big name in the world when we talk about chemical companies. SABIC is a popular chemical manufacturing company based in Saudi Arabia, their headquarters are also in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. It was founded in 1976, and for 46 years it provided its chemicals worldwide and dealt with a number of clients.

SABIC is involved in the production of many products, chemicals and intermediates, industrial polymers, fertilizers, metals, etc. They can be manufacturing and try to give the best to their customers in the world. SABIC was the fourth-ranked chemical manufacturing company in the world. SABIC generated $35.4 billion in revenue through its products in 2022.

Check their website: https://www.sabic.com/

 

5. ExxonMobil Corporation

ExxonMobil Corporation also comes in the list of top chemical manufacturing companies in the world. It is an American multinational company. The company's headquarters are in Texas, United States. It was founded in August 1882 as Standard Oil of New Jersey. It is also the second-largest oil refiner in the world according to the reports.

ExxonMobil is involved in the production of many products and chemicals such as crude oil, oil products, natural gas, petrochemicals, power generation, and other products, etc. ExxonMobil is the largest investor-owned Oil Company in the world. This company also has the sustainability in their environment to give the best to their users. ExxonMobil generated $33.7 billion in revenue through its manufactured products in 2022.

 

6. China National Chemical Corporation (ChemChina)

China National Chemical Corporation is known as ChemChina in the world. The old name of ChemChina is Bluestar Company and it was founded in 1984, 39 years ago. It is a Chinese State Owned Company. The company's headquarters are in Haidian District, Beijing, China. It is also on the list of Fortune Global 500 companies. They also have chemical sales worldwide.

ChemChina is involved in the production of many products such as agrochemicals, rubber products, chemical materials, specialty chemicals, industrial equipment, and petrochemical products, etc. ChemChina starts with a small solvent factory but now takes over 100 state-owned chemical factories across China. ChemChina has generated $28.7 billion in revenue through its manufactured products in 2022.

Check the ChemChina website for more: http://www.chemchina.com.cn/chemchinaen/

7. LyondellBasell Industries N.V.

LyondellBasell Industries is a multinational chemical manufacturing company in the world. It was formed in 2007, 16 years ago. The company headquarters are in Houston, Texas, United States, and London, UK, Rotterdam, Netherlands. It is also the third-largest independent chemical manufacturer in the United States.

LyondellBasell is involved in the production of many chemicals and polymers etc. This company is the largest licensor of polyethylene and polypropylene which is the biggest success for them. This company also comes in the top 10 chemical companies because of their sales worldwide. LyondellBasell chemical company generated $27.4 billion in revenue through its chemical products in 2022.

 

8. DuPont de Nemours, Inc.

DuPont de Nemours is an American multinational chemical manufacturing company in the world. It was formed in 1802 first, then in 2015 Dupont and the Dow Chemical Company agreed on a merger but spun off the merger in 2017. The company headquarters are in Wilmington, Delaware, USA. This company first arose as a major supplier of gunpowder. This company is also considered in the list of best chemical companies because of their products.

DuPont de Nemours is involved in the production of many polymers such as Vespel, neoprene, nylon, Corian, Teflon, Mylar, Kapton, Kevlar, Zemdrain, M5 fiber, Nomex, Tyvek, Sorona, Corfam and Lycra in the 20th century, and its scientists developed many chemicals, most notably Freon (chlorofluorocarbons), for the refrigerant industry. This company also developed synthetic pigments and paints including ChromaFlair. This chemical company generated $21.5 billion in revenue through its chemical products in 2022.

 

9. Mitsubishi Chemical Holdings Corporation

Mitsubishi Group is a Japanese multinational chemical manufacturing company in the world. It was formed in 1870, 153 years ago. The company headquarters are in Tokyo, Japan. This company provided its chemical products worldwide. In the beginning it dealt with iron supply, coal mining, to gain the coal for ships, and also started a marine insurance business.

Mitsubishi Holdings MCC is co-owner of Oji Paper Company of Yupo brand synthetic paper. This chemical company is also at the top of the list in the world because of its Group name and success factors. This chemical company generated $19.7 billion in revenue through its chemical products in 2022.

Check their website to get more: https://eu.mitsubishi-chemical.com/app/wa?account=mce

 

10. Sumitomo Chemical Co., Ltd.

Sumitomo Chemical Company is a major Japanese Chemical Company. It was founded in 1913, 109 years ago as a fertilizer manufacturing plant in Japan. The company headquarters are located in Tokyo, Japan. This company is also listed on the first section of the Tokyo Stock Exchange.

Sumitomo Chemical Company is dealing with many products, for example, Petrochemicals, Alumina products, Organic chemicals, IT-related chemicals, Agrochemicals, Pharmaceuticals, etc. It also provided a variety of chemicals and other products worldwide that's why it comes to the list of top ten chemical companies. This Sumitomo chemical company generated $17.2 billion in revenue through its chemical products in 2022.

Check Sumitomo Chemical Website to get more info: https://www.sumitomocorp.com/en/jp 

Why are these Chemical Companies in the list of Top 10?

These companies are the top ten chemical companies because of their last year revenue. They have chemical sales worldwide and earn great from their operations. Discussion about Factors contributing to the success of these top 10 chemical companies

The top chemical firms in the world have succeeded in large part due to innovation, diversity, and sustainability. So, we discuss these factors here:

1. Innovation

In the chemical sector, innovation is essential since it fosters growth and generates new commercial prospects. To create new products, enhance old ones, and streamline production procedures, businesses actively spend on research and development. Additionally, innovation enables businesses to outperform their rivals, boost productivity, cut costs, and adapt to changing client demands. The best chemical firms in the world are renowned for their innovation and put a lot of effort into creating new goods and technology.

2. Diversification

Another important element in the success of these businesses is diversification. The chemical industry is large and diversified, and businesses that operate in several niches can make use of their advantages and reduce risks. Companies can balance their portfolios through diversification and take advantage of the potential growth in other areas. The top chemical businesses in the world operate in a variety of industries, including plastics, agriculture, and specialty and basic chemicals. This offers them a competitive edge.

3. Sustainability

The chemical sector is likewise placing more and more emphasis on sustainability. Customers, investors, and regulators are pressuring businesses to operate ethically, with a reduced impact on the environment. By implementing eco-friendly processes, lowering waste and emissions, creating renewable goods, and investing in sustainable technology, the best chemical firms in the world are addressing sustainability issues. Sustainability benefits the environment as well as a business's reputation and financial bottom line.

In conclusion, sustainability, diversification, and innovation all play significant roles in the success of the best chemical firms worldwide. To stay competitive and fulfill the changing requirements of their customers, these businesses make significant investments, engage in numerous market segments, and use sustainable business practices.

Final Thoughts:

The top 10 chemical businesses in the world which we discuss, generate significant income, foster innovation, diversify their business lines, value sustainability, and are leaders in the worldwide chemical sector. These businesses have a significant global presence and operate in a variety of chemical industry sectors, such as petrochemicals, plastics, specialty chemicals, and agriculture.

They make significant investments, employ cutting-edge technology and environmentally friendly procedures, and provide a wide range of goods and services to cater to the demands of clients in numerous sectors. These elements have aided in their success and allowed them to maintain their competitiveness in the ever-changing global chemical market. They can grow if they continue to maintain their diversity and sustainability which play a huge role in their chemical sales and success.

We must remember that these chemical companies have done hard work to fulfill the needs of their customers. They can bring up innovative ideas and great chemical products so they can maintain their position in the chemical industry over the years.
